Paintball Gent has been going for more than 10 years, and has built a reputation as the prime location for indoor paintball.

Situated at a beautiful location, we’ll provide you with solid paintball gear. Our professional guidance will make for the best possible experience of this exciting sport, at a very affordable price.

Our staff commits every day to make your group activity an unforgettable experience.

Paintball Gent is very accessible thanks to our location in the Galveston-factory close to the centre of Ghent, and your paintball session can easily be combined with dinner at one of Ghent’s restaurants, a company meeting, etc. Our service is available 7 days a week, reservation only. More information can be found in the prices/reservation section on our site. In case of any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us via phone or e-mail (cf. Contact).

Whether you’re looking for a group activity to celebrate your birthday, have some teambuilding or hold a stag or hen party, Paintball Gent is the perfect place for you! Do not hesitate to book your amazing paintball session right now!

For the younger generation, we have our popular children’s paintball sessions which makes every teen’s birthday unforgettable.

Assets

+ Exclusive group experience

You always play with only your friends or colleagues; we never mix different groups. You have our undivided attention, as to guarantee you a more than smooth session.

+ Democratic prices

We give you a top notch experience for an affordable price.

+ More than 10 years of experience

We know what we’re doing, and we know what our clients need for a great experience.

+ Indoor location

Our gigantic and entertaining indoor hall ensures your activity always has a green light. Whether rain or shine, summer or winter: at Paintball Gent it’s always bright weather!

+ High quality gear

We work with quality gear, i.e. the equipment that we would like to play with ourselves as a customer.

+ Professional guidance

Our team will guide your group from beginning to end and will guarantee a smooth and safe experience.

+ High customer satisfaction

As a local family business, you are not a number to us. We sincerely believe it’s important that all our customers have a great time with us.

+ Close to the centre of Ghent

There are infinite possibilities in Ghent for your group to have a drink or a bite after your session. We will gladly give our recommendations.
